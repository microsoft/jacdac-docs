# Error Unknown micro:bit hardware revision

## The problem

We were unable to determine the hardware revision of your micro:bit. This is probably an issue with your firmware on the micro:bit. 

## How to fix it

Try updating the firmware on your micro:bit by following these instructions.

* [Upgrade the firmware on the micro:bit](https://support.microbit.org/support/solutions/articles/19000019131-upgrade-the-firmware-on-the-micro-bit)
